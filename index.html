UNIT 1B

Adjectives (Sıfatlar)

One/ones · Comparatives · Superlatives · A bit / Much

1. Sıfat Nedir? (What is an adjective?)

Sıfat; yer, kişi veya eşyaları tanımlayan/niteleyen kelimedir. Cümlede isimden önce veya fiilden sonra gelir.

İsimden önce (Adjective + Noun): She has a big house.

Fiilden sonra (Verb + Adjective): Suzan is beautiful. ✅

⚠️ Sıfat + İsim kullanıldığında A/AN şart!

❌ Suzan is a beautiful girl. → She is a beautiful girl. ✅

2. One / Ones

Tekrar söylemekten kaçınmak için sayılabilir isimler yerine one (tekil) ve ones (çoğul) kullanılır.

Tekil isim yerine →

ONE

Çoğul isim yerine →

ONES
  • A: Which pen? B: The blue one. (pen = tekil → one)
  • A: Which books? B: The red ones. (books = çoğul → ones)

3. Comparatives (Karşılaştırma)

İki şeyi, kişiyi veya yeri kıyaslarken kullanılır.

KuralÖrnek
1 heceli sıfat → -er + thantall → taller than
2+ heceli sıfat → more + thanexpensive → more expensive than
"-ed" ile biten → morebored → more bored ✅
İstisna: good / bad / farbetter / worse / farther
⚠️ Kritik Kural — "-ed" Sıfatları:
Kısa olsalar da more kullanılır!
bored → more bored · tired → more tired · stressed → more stressed · pleased → more pleased · shocked → more shocked

4. Superlatives (En üstünlük)

Bir grupta "en…" olanı söylemek için kullanılır. Daima the ile gelir.

KuralÖrnek
1 heceli → the + -esttall → the tallest
2+ heceli → the mostbeautiful → the most beautiful
"-ed" sıfatlar → the moststressed → the most stressed
narrow, clever → her ikisi de oknarrower / the narrowest

5. A bit & Much + Comparative

A bit + comparative = küçük bir fark var → Tom is a bit taller than Terry.

Much + comparative = büyük bir fark var → iPhones are much more expensive than Redmi.

📝 Mini Quiz: Adjectives

1. Which sentence is correct?

2. My new phone is ___ more expensive than my old one. (Fark çok büyük!)

3. "tired" kelimesinin superlative hali nedir?

UNIT 2B

Possessives (İyelik)

's · Plural possessives · Two names · Of · Own

1. Possessive 's

Lara's backpack is on the chair.

This is Tom's car.

2. Çoğul İyelik

İsim zaten "s" ile bitiyorsa sadece ' eklenir: The teachers' room is upstairs.

"s" ile bitmeyen düzensiz çoğullara 's eklenir: The children's playground · The women's team

3. İki İsim (Two Names)

Paylaşılan bir şey → sadece ikinci isme 's:
Emma and Mia's house is modern. ✅

Ayrı ayrı şeyler → her iki isme de 's:
Emma's and Mia's bags are different.

4. "Of" Yapısı

The door of the car · The end of the movie · The top of the building

5. Own

She has her own room. · They started their own company.

📝 Mini Quiz: Possessives

1. Birden fazla öğretmenin odası → "The ___ room is on the left."

2. Emma ve Mia ortak bir ev paylaşıyor → hangisi doğru?

3. "The door of the car" — Neden 's kullanmıyoruz?

UNIT 5A

Present Perfect Simple

have/has + V3 · for/since · just/yet/already · experiences

Yapı (Structure)

Olumlu →

have / has + V3 (past participle)

Olumsuz →

haven't / hasn't + V3

Soru →

Have/Has + subject + V3 ?

Ne Zaman Kullanılır?

KullanımSinyal KelimelerÖrnek
Geçmiş deneyimlerever, neverHave you ever been to Spain?
Yeni biten eylemlerjustShe's just sent a text.
Henüz olmamış/beklenenyetI haven't finished yet.
Beklenenden erken olanalreadyI've already finished!
Sürefor, sinceI've lived here for 3 years.

FOR vs SINCE

FOR + süre → for two minutes / for ten years / for a long time

SINCE + başlangıç noktası → since this morning / since 5.00 / since 2010

Stative Verbs ile Kullanım

⚠️ Önemli: know, like, love, want, need gibi fiiller PP Continuous ALAMAZ!

✅ I've known him for 5 years.
❌ I've been knowing him for 5 years.

Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Zaman kelimesi varsa → KESİNLİKLE Past Simple!

✅ I watched Netflix last weekend. (Past Simple)
✅ I've watched Netflix a few times. (Present Perfect)

📝 Mini Quiz: Present Perfect Simple

1. Which is correct?

2. "I finished it ___ yesterday." — Hangi zaman?

3. Sally has known him ___ 20 years.

UNIT 5B

Present Perfect Continuous

have/has been + V-ing · recently/lately · görünür sonuçlar

Yapı (Structure)

Olumlu →

have / has + been + V-ing

Olumsuz →

haven't / hasn't + been + V-ing

Ne Zaman Kullanılır?

KullanımSinyal KelimelerÖrnek
Hala devam eden tekrarlanan eylemlerrecently, latelyShe's been going for a walk every morning this week.
Taze/görünür fiziksel sonuç—You look tired. I've been working in the garden.
Sürmekte olan eylemall day, all weekIt's been raining all day.
Geçmişten şimdiye sürenfor, sinceI've been waiting since 10 o'clock.

Simple vs Continuous

Present Perfect Simple → Eylem bitti, sonucu önemli: I've painted the kitchen.

Present Perfect Continuous → Eylem devam ediyor ya da taze iz bıraktı: I've been painting the kitchen.

⚠️ Stative Verbs Continuous ALAMAZ!
✅ I've known them for 10 years. ❌ I've been knowing them.

İstisna: work ve live — her ikisi de kullanılabilir:
I've been living here. = I've lived here. ✅

📝 Mini Quiz: Present Perfect Continuous

1. A: You look tired! B: I know. I ___ (work) in the garden.

2. How long ___ you been looking for a new job?

3. John ___ (study) for his exam lately — he looks exhausted.

UNIT 4B

1st & 2nd Conditionals

Gerçek gelecek olasılıklar ve hayali durumlar

1st Conditional

Yapı →

If + present simple, will / won't + infinitive

If I have time, I'll write my CV tonight. ✅

If you don't work hard, you won't get promoted. ✅

2nd Conditional

Yapı →

If + past simple, would / wouldn't + infinitive

If I had more money, I wouldn't need to work overtime. ✅

If John was/were here, he'd know what to do. ✅

Unless (= if…not)

I won't go unless you go too. = I won't go if you don't go too.
⚠️ "If I were you…" → were, was değil!
✅ If I were you, I'd apologize. ❌ If I was you…

📝 Mini Quiz: 1st & 2nd Conditionals

1. If she ___ (have) her own car, she wouldn't need to borrow yours. (Hayali)

2. If you get a good degree, you ___ find a better job. (Gerçekçi olasılık)

3. I won't help ___ you tell me what the problem is.

UNIT 9B

3rd Conditional & Past Perfect

Geçmişe dair hayaller · Past perfect diğer kullanımlar

3rd Conditional

Yapı →

If + past perfect (had + V3), would have + V3

If I'd known his number, I would have called him. ✅

What would we have done if we'd missed the flight? ✅

⚠️ 'd = had mi, would mi?
If I'd known → If I had known (if clause'da HAD)
I'd have called → I would have called (sonuç cümlesinde WOULD)

Past Perfect — Diğer Kullanımlar

Yapı →

had + V3
KullanımÖrnek
Geçmişte iki olay — daha önceki içinWhen we arrived, the flight had already closed.
Reported speech'de backshift"I've finished." → She said she had finished.

📝 Mini Quiz: 3rd Conditional

1. If I ___ (know) his number, I would have called him.

2. If they hadn't gone to the wedding, they ___ met.

3. When we arrived at the gate, the flight ___ already ___. (close)

UNIT 6A

Modals: Obligation, Advice

have to · must · needn't · should · ought to

1. Obligation & Necessity

ModalNe Zaman?Örnek
have toDışarıdan gelen kural/zorunlulukI have to start work at 9.00.
mustKişisel zorunluluk. Yalnızca present tense'te.I must remember to book a table.
need toGerekli olan şeylerI need to buy some food tonight.

2. No Obligation

don't have to = zorunluluk yok → You don't have to pay me now.

needn't = zorunluluk yok → You needn't hurry. We have plenty of time.

3. Prohibition

mustn't = kesin yasak!
→ You mustn't drive. You've been drinking.

⚠️ mustn't ≠ don't have to!
mustn't = YASAK · don't have to = zorunlu değil

4. Advice

ModalAçıklamaÖrnek
should / shouldn'tTavsiyeYou should try that restaurant.
ought toshould ile aynı anlamShe oughtn't to spend so much.
must / have to (tavsiye)Güçlü tavsiyeYou must try the coffee there!

📝 Mini Quiz: Modals

1. You ___ drive! You've been drinking. (Kesin yasak)

2. You ___ come early — zorunlu değil.

3. "I ___ remember to call her." — kişisel karar, present tense

UNIT 6B

Can, Could & Be able to

Yetenek · Olasılık · İzin · Çıkarım (Deduction)

1. Yetenek / Olasılık / İzin

ModalKullanımÖrnek
canŞimdiki yetenek, izin, olasılıkYou can use a toothbrush to clean jewellery.
can'tYetenek yok, yasak, imkansızWe can't park here.
couldGeçmişteki genel yetenekShe could swim when she was three.
be able toDiğer tüm zaman ve formlarI've been able to drive since I was 17.

2. Geçmişte Tek Bir Durumda Başarı

⚠️ Tek ve Zor Bir Olayda COULD Kullanılmaz!
✅ I couldn't find it, but I was able to buy it online.
❌ I could buy it online. (tek başarı için could olmaz)

3. Deduction (Çıkarım)

can't = imkansız → It can't be broken! I only bought it last week.

must = kesin doğru → She must be at work now. It's 9.30 a.m.

📝 Mini Quiz: Can / Could / Be able to

1. I couldn't find a taxi, but in the end I ___ get one. (Tek, zor durum)

2. I'd love ___ ski. (yetenek, gelecek hayali)

3. It ___ be true! She'd never do that. (İmkansız çıkarım)

UNIT 7A

Phrasal Verbs

Type 1 · Type 2 (separable) · Type 3 (inseparable)

Type 1 — Nesnesi Olmayan

I get up at 6.00. · They set off early. · The plane took off late.

Type 2 — Ayrılabilen (Separable)

Can you fill in this form? = Can you fill this form in? ✅

⚠️ ZAMİR KURALI — EN ÖNEMLİ KURAL!
Nesne bir ZAMİRse → DAIMA fiil ile parçacığın ARASINA girer:
✅ Did you switch it off? ❌ Did you switch off it?
✅ Please put them away. ❌ Please put away them.

Type 3 — Ayrılamayan (Inseparable)

My sister and I both take after our father. → We take after him. ✅

Jane's looking forward to her holiday.

UNIT 7B

Verb Patterns

to + infinitive · bare infinitive · gerund (-ing)

1. Infinitive with TO

agree, decide, want, need, try, manage, plan, hope, refuse, afford…

→ Mandy agreed to come. · She refused to help.

2. Bare Infinitive

Modal'lardan sonra: → He can speak five languages.

let / make + nesne: → His parents let him go. · She made me wait.

3. Gerund (-ing)

enjoy, finish, hate, like, love, mind, avoid, give up, carry on, look forward to…

→ They enjoy watching films. · She's given up working on Saturdays.

4. Verb + Object + Infinitive

want, tell, ask, allow, persuade + nesne + to + infinitive:

→ They want us to go. · Liam told me not to tell.

Özet Tablo

YapıFiiller
verb + to + infwant, need, decide, agree, try, refuse, manage, hope
verb + -ingenjoy, finish, hate, mind, avoid, give up, keep, carry on
verb + obj + to + infwant, tell, ask, allow, persuade, teach, remind, warn
verb + obj + bare inflet, make (+ object)
modal + bare infcan, could, will, would, must, should, may, might

📝 Mini Quiz: Verb Patterns

1. They enjoy ___ (watch) films at home.

2. His parents let him ___ (go) to the concert.

3. I need ___ (go) to the supermarket.

UNIT 8A

Have Something Done

Causative yapı · Başkasına yaptırtmak

Yapı ve Anlam

Yapı →

have + object + past participle (V3)

I cleaned my car yesterday. → Ben kendim temizledim.

I had my car cleaned yesterday. → Birine temizlettim.

I'm having my hair cut tomorrow. → Saçımı kestireceğim.

Tüm Zamanlarla Kullanım

ZamanÖrnek
Present SimpleHow often do you have your car serviced?
Present ContinuousI'm having my hair cut tomorrow.
ModalYou ought to have your roof repaired.
⚠️ "get" de kullanılabilir!
I'm going to have my hair cut. = I'm going to get my hair cut. ✅

UNIT 8B

The Passive Voice

Tüm zamanların passive halleri · Active ↔ Passive dönüşüm

Passive Yapı

Temel yapı →

am / is / are / was / were / been + V3

Tüm Zamanlarda Passive

ZamanPassive YapıÖrnek
Present Simpleis/are + V3The site is visited by thousands.
Present Continuousis/are + being + V3The castle is being restored.
Present Perfecthas/have + been + V3The King's body has never been found.
Past Simplewas/were + V310,000 soldiers were killed in an hour.
Past Continuouswas/were + being + V3The bridge was being repaired.
Past Perfecthad + been + V3The hospital had been opened by the Queen.
will / going towill be + V3When will the museum be built?
Infinitive with toto + be + V3The city has to be closed.
Gerundbeing + V3Most people hate being woken up.
⚠️ Kim yaptığını söylemek istersen → "by" kullan:

UNIT 10A

Be, Do & Have

Ana fiil ve yardımcı fiil olarak kullanımları

BE

KullanımÖrnek
Ana fiil (olmak)I was very sensitive when I was a child.
Yardımcı: Present ContinuousI'm sitting on the bus.
Yardımcı: Passive (tüm zamanlar)My car is being repaired.

DO

KullanımÖrnek
Ana fiil (yapmak)I did my homework.
Yardımcı: Present Simple soru/olumsuzShe doesn't speak English.

HAVE

KullanımÖrnek

⚠️ Dikkat: Sınav Tuzakları!

Aşağıdaki karşılaştırmalar sınavda en çok hata yapılan konulardır.

İkiliEn Kritik FarkDoğru Örnek
PP Simple vs Past Simple Zaman kelimesi (yesterday, last week, ago) → Past Simple! Just/yet/already → PP Simple. I finished it yesterday. (PS)
I've just finished it. (PP)
PP Simple vs PP Continuous Simple → eylem bitti. Continuous → devam ediyor veya taze iz. I've painted the kitchen. (bitti)
I've been painting — I'm covered in paint!
mustn't vs don't have to mustn't = YASAK · don't have to = zorunluluk yok. You mustn't smoke here!
You don't have to come early.
will vs be going to will = anlık karar, kanıtsız tahmin. going to = önceden planlanmış, kanıtlı tahmin. I'll answer the phone!
It's going to rain. (bulutlar var)
1st vs 2nd Conditional 1st = gerçek olasılık. 2nd = hayali durum. If I have time, I'll help.
If I had time, I'd help.
could vs was/were able to could = genel yetenek. was/were able to = tek, zor bir olayda başarı. She could swim at 3.
I was able to buy it online.
Type 2 vs Type 3 Phrasal Verbs Type 2 = ayrılabilir (zamir araya girer!). Type 3 = hiç ayrılamaz. Turn it off. ✅
We take after him. ✅
Causative vs Perfect Causative = birine yaptırtmak. Perfect = zaman yardımcısı. I've had my car fixed. (yaptırttım)
I've had breakfast. (yedim)
to+inf vs -ing want/need/decide → to. enjoy/finish/give up → -ing. let/make+obj → bare inf! I want to go.
I enjoy going.
She let him go.

📌 Stative verbs ASLA -ing almaz!
know, want, need, like, love, believe, hate, prefer → Simple kullanılır.

"Where do you live?" → She asked me where I lived. ✅
📌 Geçmişte tek başarı → was/were able to!
Tek bir zor olayda could değil was/were able to kullanılır.
📌 mustn't ≠ don't have to!
mustn't = YASAK. don't have to = zorunlu değil.
📌 Causative = have + obj + V3
"I had my car washed" = yaptırttım, kendim değil.
📌 "If I were you…" → were, was değil!
If I were you, I'd apologize. ✅
📌 "-ed" sıfatlar → more / the most!
tired → more tired ✅ · bored → the most bored ✅
📌 let / make + obj + bare infinitive!
His parents let him go. ✅ · She made me wait. ✅
📌 Past Perfect = daha eski geçmiş
When we arrived, the flight had already closed.
📌 Passive'de yapan → "by" ile!
The building was designed by a famous architect.
📌 for = süre, since = başlangıç noktası
for two years · since 2010 · since Monday
📌 3rd Conditional = geçmiş hayal
If + past perfect → would have + V3. Pişmanlık/hayal.
📌 PP Simple → sonuç önemli
I've painted the kitchen. (bitti, sonuç var)
📌 PP Continuous → devam / taze iz
I've been painting — I'm covered in paint! (iz var)
