UNIT 1B

Adjectives (Sıfatlar)

One/ones · Comparatives · Superlatives · A bit / Much

1. Sıfat Kullanımı

⚠️ Sıfat + İsim kullanıldığında tekillerde A/AN şarttır!

She is a beautiful girl. ✅ (NOT She is beautiful girl ❌)

2. One / Ones

Tekrarı önlemek için kullanılır. Tekil isim yerine one, çoğul yerine ones.

A: Which pen? B: The blue one. (pen = singular)

A: Which books? B: The red ones. (books = plural)

3. Comparatives & Superlatives

AdjectiveComparative (Kıyas)Superlative (En)
tall (tek hece)taller thanthe tallest
bored (-ed sıfatlar)more bored thanthe most bored
dangerous (çok hece)more dangerousthe most dangerous
good (düzensiz)betterthe best
⚠️ A bit & Much
Küçük fark → It's a bit cloudier today.
Büyük fark → Your job is much more stressful than mine.

UNIT 2A

Present Tenses

Action / Non-Action Verbs · Future Arrangements · Timetables

1. Action vs Non-Action (Stative) Verbs

Bazı fiiller durum, his veya sahiplik bildirir. Bu fiiller -ing almazlar! (Present Continuous ile KULLANILMAZLAR)

Stative Verbs:

agree, believe, belong, hate, know, like, love, need, prefer, realize, remember, suppose, want
⚠️ Dikkat: Anlama Göre Değişenler!
HAVE: I have a car (Sahiplik = Stative). / I am having a shower (Eylem = Action).
THINK: I think it's a good idea (Fikir). / I am thinking about moving (Düşünme eylemi).

2. Gelecek Planları (Future Arrangements)

Kişisel olarak planlanmış ve ayarlanmış gelecek olayları için Present Continuous (am/is/are + V-ing) kullanılır.

I'm leaving tomorrow and I'm coming back on Tuesday.

We're seeing our grandparents this weekend.

3. Timetable Future (Zaman Çizelgeleri)

Ulaşım (uçak, tren vb.), sinema, ders saatleri gibi kişisel olmayan sabit zaman çizelgeleri için Present Simple kullanılır.

The train leaves at 6.30 in the morning.

What time does your flight arrive?

📝 Workbook Practice: Present Tenses

1. The flight ________ at 6.50 in the morning.

2. We ________ in an airport hotel tonight. (£200 a night!)

3. ________ Andrew's sister?

4. I ________ of going on a safari next year.

UNIT 2B

Possessives (İyelik)

's · Plural possessives · Two names · Of · Own

1. Possessive 's & Çoğul İyelik

Tekil: This is Tom's car.

Düzenli Çoğul (-s ile biten): The teachers' room is upstairs.

Düzensiz Çoğul: The children's playground.

2. İki İsim (Two Names)

Paylaşılan tek bir şeyse → sadece ikinci isme 's: Emma and Mia's house is modern. ✅
Ayrı ayrı şeylerse → her ikisine 's: Emma's and Mia's bags are different. ✅

3. "Of" Yapısı

Cansız nesnelerin sahipliği için kullanılır.

The door of the car was open. (NOT the car's door)

The top of the building. (NOT the building's top)

📝 Workbook Practice: Possessives

1. What's ________ where Suzy works?

2. That's ________ over there.

3. ________ names are Peter and Michael.

UNIT 3A

Past Tenses & Used To

Past Simple · Past Continuous · Past Habits

1. Past Simple

Geçmişte tamamlanmış eylemler için kullanılır. (Verb 2)

When I was young, I loved playing outside.

Where did you go to school? (NOT did you went ❌)

2. Past Continuous

Geçmişte belli bir anda devam eden veya başka bir eylem tarafından bölünen eylemler için.

was / were + V-ing

I was watching TV when you arrived.

He was texting a friend when the accident happened. (Uzun eylem continuous, kısa eylem simple)

3. Used to

Geçmişteki alışkanlıklar veya şu an geçerli olmayan uzun süreli durumlar için.

✅ I used to live in Rome.
❌ They didn't used to live in London. (Doğrusu: didn't use to)
⚠️ Ne kadar süre yapıldığını belirtiyorsan "used to" kullanılmaz! (NOT I used to live in Rome for ten years ❌ -> I lived in Rome for ten years ✅)

📝 Workbook Practice: Past Tenses

1. Where did you ________ on holiday when you were young?

2. This time last week I ________ on a beach.

3. We used to ________ going to concerts when we were students.

4. Sorry, I didn't hear what you said, I ________ the news.

UNIT 3B

Prepositions (Edatlar)

Place · Movement · Dependent Prepositions

1. Place & Movement

KategoriEdatlar & Örnekler
Place (Yer)in, on, under, in front of. She sat in the square.
Movement (Hareket)over, across, through, along. The plane flew over the city.

2. Dependent Prepositions

wait for, worried about, good at, tired of, proud of.

⚠️ Edattan Sonra -ING Gelir!
I'm looking forward to seeing you. (NOT to see)

⚠️ Edat ALMAYAN Fiiller (Sınav Tuzağı):
ask, discuss, enter, marry, tell → We discussed the situation. (NOT discussed about)

📝 Workbook Practice: Prepositions

2. I'm tired ________ all this work - I'm ready ________ a holiday!

3. We need to discuss ________ the problems with our IT department.

UNIT 4A

Future Forms

Will / Shall · Be Going To · Future in the Past

Will / Shall vs Be Going To

FormKullanım AlanıÖrnek
will / won'tAnlık kararlar, sözler, kontrol dışı gerçekler.I'll have a coffee. / I won't do it again.
be going toÖnceden yapılmış planlar, kanıta dayalı tahminler.Look at the clouds! It's going to rain.
shall I / weTeklif ve öneri sormak için (sadece I ve We ile).Shall I cook dinner tonight?
⚠️ The Future in the Past (Geçmişte Gelecek)
Gerçekleşmeyen planlar için was/were going to kullanılır.
I was going to call you, but I forgot.

UNIT 4B

1st & 2nd Conditionals

Gerçek Olasılıklar vs Hayali Durumlar

1st Conditional (Gerçekçi Gelecek)

If + Present Simple , will/won't + V1

If I have time, I'll write my CV tonight.

2nd Conditional (Hayali Durumlar)

If + Past Simple , would/wouldn't + V1

If I had more money, I wouldn't need to work overtime.

⚠️ Tavsiye verirken: If I were you... (was değil!)
Unless = If... not: I won't go unless you go too. (=if you don't go)

📝 Workbook Practice: 1st & 2nd Cond.

1. If I go to university, I ________ engineering.

2. If she ________ her own car, she wouldn't need to borrow yours.

3. We can't help you ________ you tell us what the problem is.

UNIT 5A

Present Perfect Simple

Have/has + V3 · For/Since · Just/Yet/Already

Ne Zaman Kullanılır?

KullanımÖrnek
Geçmiş deneyimler (zaman belirsiz)Have you ever watched a foreign TV series?
Yeni olmuş, taze haber (just)He's just sent me a text.
Beklenenden erken (already)I've already finished it.
Henüz olmamış / Soru (yet)I haven't talked to her yet.
⚠️ Geçmiş Zaman Kelimesi Varsa → Past Simple!
✅ I watched six episodes last weekend.
❌ I have watched six episodes last weekend.

UNIT 5B

Present Perfect Continuous

Have been + V-ing · Süreklilik ve Görünür Sonuçlar

Kullanım Alanları

1. Tekrarlanan ve devam eden eylemler (lately / recently):
She's been going for a walk every morning this week.

2. Gözle görülür fiziksel sonuç bırakan eylemler:
A: You look tired. B: I've been working in the garden. (Eylem bitmiş olsa da izi taze)

⚠️ Stative Verbs Continuous Olmaz!
I've known them for 10 years. (NOT I've been knowing them ❌)

Work ve Live İstisnası:
Bu iki fiil Simple ve Continuous ile aynı anlamda kullanılabilir.
I've lived here = I've been living here.

📝 Workbook Practice: Perfect Continuous

1. I ________ too hard lately.

2. Ania is really tired - she ________ a lot for work since February.

3. At last! I ________ for you for an hour!

UNIT 6A

Modals

Obligation, Necessity, Prohibition, Advice

TürModalsÖrnek
Obligation (Zorunluluk)have to, must, need toI have to start work at 9.00. (Dış kural)
I must remember... (İç zorunluluk)
No Obligation (Gereklilik Yok)don't have to, needn'tYou don't have to pay me now.
Prohibition (Yasak!)mustn'tYou mustn't drive. You've been drinking.
Advice (Tavsiye)should, ought toYou should try that restaurant.
⚠️ En Büyük Sınav Tuzağı: mustn't vs don't have to
mustn't: Yapılması tehlikeli veya yasak! (Don't drive!)
don't have to: Yapmak zorunda değilsin, istersen yap.

📝 Workbook Practice: Modals

1. I ________ buy a new fridge last week.

2. We ________ be at the airport until 5.00. Our flight isn't until 7.00.

3. You ________ spill anything on the sofa - it's leather.

UNIT 6B

Can, Could, Be able to

Ability, Possibility, Permission, Deduction

Temel Kurallar

Can: Şimdiki yetenek veya izin. (I can sew.)
Could: Geçmişteki genel yetenek. (She could swim when she was three.)
Be able to: Can'in olmadığı zaman formlarında kullanılır (Future, Perfect, Infinitive). (I'd love to be able to ski.)

⚠️ Geçmişte Tek, Spesifik Bir Olayda Başarı!
Geçmişte başardığımız zor, tekil bir olay için could kullanılmaz! was/were able to veya managed to kullanılır.
I couldn't find the book, but in the end I was able to buy it online. ✅ (NOT I could buy it ❌)

📝 Workbook Practice: Can / Could / Able to

1. I'm afraid it's broken and I ________ mend it.

2. He loves music - he ________ play the violin when he was four!

3. I got a puncture, but I ________ change the wheel myself.

UNIT 7A

Phrasal Verbs

Type 1 (No object) · Type 2 (Separable) · Type 3 (Inseparable)

1. Type 1: No Object

Nesne almazlar, parçalanamazlar. (get up, set off, take off)

2. Type 2: Separable (Ayrılabilir)

Nesne alan ve fiil ile edatı arasına nesne girebilen phrasal verb'lerdir. (fill in, put away, switch off)

⚠️ En Kritik Kural: ZAMİR KURALI!
Eğer nesne bir zamirse (it, them), DAİMA fiil ile edatın arasına girmek zorundadır.
✅ I switched it off.
❌ I switched off it.

3. Type 3: Inseparable (Ayrılamaz)

Nesne almalarına rağmen asla ayrılamazlar. Zamir gelse bile sona gelir. (take after, get on with, look forward to)
✅ We take after him. (NOT We take him after ❌)

📝 Workbook Practice: Phrasal Verbs

1. The pasta was cold, so I ________.

2. They ________.

3. Your phone's ringing. Quick, ________ ! (turn off)

UNIT 7B

Verb Patterns

Infinitive with TO · Bare Infinitive · Gerund (-ing)

FormYaygın FiillerÖrnek
+ TO (Infinitive)want, need, try, decide, hope, refuseMandy agreed to come.
+ -ING (Gerund)enjoy, finish, hate, give up, avoidThey enjoy watching films.
Bare (Yalın)can, must, should (modals)He can speak five languages.
⚠️ Let ve Make Kuralları (Sınav Tuzağı!)
"Let" ve "Make" fiilleri kendilerinden sonra gelen nesneyle TO almadan yalın kullanılır!
✅ Our boss makes us work late. (NOT to work)
✅ His parents let him go to the concert. (NOT to go)

📝 Workbook Practice: Verb Patterns

UNIT 8A

Have Something Done

Başkasına İş Yaptırmak (Causative)

HAVE + OBJECT (Nesne) + V3 (Past Participle)

İşi kendimiz yapmadığımızda, paramızla veya rica ederek başkasına yaptırdığımızda bu yapıyı kullanırız.

Kendim yaptım: I cleaned my car yesterday.

Yaptırttım: I had my car cleaned yesterday. (= I paid someone to clean it)

Not: "Have" yerine "Get" de kullanılabilir. (I'm going to get my hair cut.)

UNIT 8B

The Passive

Edilgen Çatı (Am/is/are/was/were/been + V3)

Eylemi yapan kişi bilinmediğinde, önemli olmadığında veya asıl odak eylemin kendisiyse Passive (Edilgen) yapı kullanılır.

ZamanYapıÖrnek
Present Simpleis / are + V3The site is visited by thousands.
Present Continuousis / are being + V3The castle is being restored.
Past Simplewas / were + V310,000 soldiers were killed.
Present Perfecthas / have been + V3The body has never been found.
Willwill be + V3When will the museum be built?
Eylemi kimin yaptığını vurgulamak istersen cümlenin sonunda by kullanırsın.
The Sagrada Familia was designed by Antoni Gaudi.

📝 Workbook Practice: Passive

1. The Guggenheim Museum in Bilbao ________ in 1997. (open)

2. A new shopping centre ________ in the town centre at the moment. (build)

3. They grow rice in Valencia. (Active) -> Passive:

UNIT 9A

Reported Speech

Dolaylı Anlatım · Statements · Questions · Imperatives

1. Statements (Zaman Kayması - Backshift)

Doğrudan Söylem (Direct)Dolaylı Aktarım (Reported)
Present Simple (I have)Past Simple (He said he had)
Present Cont. (I am leaving)Past Cont. (He said he was leaving)
Past Simple / PerfectPast Perfect (He said he had seen)
will / can / mustwould / could / had to

2. Reported Questions (Sınav Tuzağı!)

⚠️ Soruları aktarırken:
1. DO / DOES / DID kullanılmaz!
2. Soru işareti kullanılmaz!
3. Özne fiilden önce gelir (Düz cümle sırası).
"Where do you live?" → She asked me where I lived. ✅ (NOT where did I live ❌)
Evet/Hayır sorularında if / whether kullanılır.
"Are you coming?" → She asked him if he was coming.

3. Imperatives (Emir/Rica)

"Wait in the car" → She told me to wait in the car.
"Don't forget" → He told me not to forget.

📝 Workbook Practice: Reported Speech

1. "I can't find my purse." → She said (that) ________.

2. "Where do you live?" → He asked me ________.

UNIT 9B

3rd Conditional & Past Perfect

Geçmişte Hayali Durumlar · Past Perfect

Third Conditional (Pişmanlık / Geçmiş Hayal)

If + Past Perfect (had V3) , would have + V3

If I'd known his number, I would have called him.

What would we have done if we'd missed the flight?

Past Perfect (had + V3) Kullanımları

Geçmişte yaşanmış iki olaydan daha önce olanı belirtmek için kullanılır.

When we arrived at the departure gate, the flight had already closed. (Biz gelmeden önce kapanmıştı)

📝 Workbook Practice: 3rd Conditional

1. If we ________ the bus, we ________ home till midnight. (miss, not get)

2. If they ________ to the wedding, they ________ . (invite, go)

3. When she woke up, the house was empty - he ________ . (go)

UNIT 10A

Be, Do & Have

Main Verbs vs Auxiliary Verbs

İngilizcedeki en temel üç fiil hem "Ana Fiil" (Main Verb) hem de "Yardımcı Fiil" (Auxiliary) olarak kullanılabilir.

FiilMain Verb OlarakAuxiliary (Yardımcı) Olarak
BEI was very sensitive. (Durum)I'm sitting on the bus. (Continuous)
My car has been stolen. (Passive)
DOI did my homework. (Yapmak)She doesn't speak English. (Olumsuz/Soru)
HAVEI have a sister. (Sahiplik)
I'm having a shower. (Eylem)		They've bought a new car. (Perfect Tense)

⚖️ Karışanlar Arenası

Sınavda en çok tuzağa düşülen ikililer

İkiliEn Kritik FarkDoğru Örnek
will vs be going towill = anlık karar. going to = plan / kanıtlı tahmin.I'll get it! / I'm going to start a course.
towards vs totowards = hedefe doğru (ulaşmayabilir). to = ulaştı.The dog ran towards me. / The dog ran to me.
PP Simple vs Past SimpleZaman kelimesi (yesterday, last week) → Past Simple!I finished it yesterday. / I've just finished it.
mustn't vs don't have tomustn't = YASAK · don't have to = zorunluluk yok.You mustn't smoke here! / You don't have to come early.
1st vs 2nd Conditional1st = gerçek olasılık. 2nd = hayali durum.If I have time, I'll help. / If I had time, I'd help.
could vs was able tocould = genel yetenek. was able to = tek zor olayda başarı.She could swim at 3. / I was able to buy it online.
to+inf vs -ingwant/need → to. enjoy/hate → -ing. let/make+obj → bare inf!I want to go. / I enjoy going. / She let him go.

⚡ Sınav Öncesi Hızlı Tekrar

Sınava son 15 dakika kala mutlaka gözden geçir!

📌 Stative verbs ASLA -ing almaz!
know, want, need, like, love, believe, hate, prefer.
📌 Phrasal verb + zamir → ARASINA girer!
Turn it off ✅ · Turn off it ❌
📌 Reported questions → do/did KULLANILMAZ!
"Where do you live?" → She asked me where I lived. ✅
📌 Geçmişte tek başarı → was/were able to!
Tek bir zor olayda could değil was able to kullanılır.
📌 mustn't ≠ don't have to!
mustn't = YASAK. don't have to = istersen yap.
📌 Causative = have + obj + V3
"I had my car washed" = yaptırttım, kendim değil.
📌 "If I were you…" → were, was değil!
If I were you, I'd apologize. ✅
📌 let / make + obj + bare infinitive!
His parents let him go. (to go değil)
📌 Edat sonrası → -ing!
looking forward to seeing · tired of waiting
📌 was/were going to = gerçekleşmeyen plan
I was going to visit but he was abroad.
